Request Account Deletion

What data will be deleted: Your account, pet profiles, appointment history, chat messages, and uploaded photos will be permanently removed.

Data Retention

Upon account deletion, the following data will be permanently deleted:

  • Account information (name, email, phone number)
  • Pet profiles and photos
  • Appointment history
  • Chat messages
  • All uploaded images

Deletion is irreversible. Please make sure you no longer need access to your account before submitting a request.
